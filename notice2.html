TOYS INNOVATION CHALLENGE

NOTICE

The 'AATMANIRBHAR TOYS INNOVATION CHALLENGE' welcomes you to participate and create an attractive toy based game, inspired by Indian tradition and culture. Toys and games have always been an enjoyable means of training young children about life and values in society. Through this innovation challenge, we will be wish to educate children about Indian culture, heritage and rituals by immersing them into our cultural richness in a fun, interactive way. This is the time that we utilize the strength of our bright ideas and bring out games based on our Indian culture, tradition and heritage and stories of ancient lives and put into display the beliefs and traditions that exist among communities of people. For more details, please visit: https://innovateindia.mygov.in/toy-hackathon/
